    STEP creates secure communications channels between applications

their partners.

     Easy to interface with SAP and other ERP systems.
     XML-compatible for e-commerce applications.
     Uses an extensible framework for custom middleware applications.
     Strong encryption keeps your data confidential and tamper-proof.
     Portable to all your platforms: Windows, Linux, Unix, OS/390, and 
more.
     Based on open security standards - RSA, OpenSSL, and more.


    Strategy:   

    Use public and private Internet links to create the fastest possible 
data exchange channel between two or more applications.
    Fact:
    
    STEP requires no public-facing web servers - STEP applications 
remain fully protected behind your company firewall.        
    STEP's security model is configurable, extensible, and based on open 
security standards.        
    A typical STEP application: an electronic payments system for one of 
our customers in Africa.
